Two Ingredient Pizza Dough

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups self-rising flour, plus more for kneading
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Mix flour and Greek yogurt together in a bowl; transfer to a work surface floured with self-rising flour. Knead dough, adding more flour as needed to keep dough from being too sticky, for 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Spray a 12-inch pizza pan with cooking spray and spread dough to edges of pan.
